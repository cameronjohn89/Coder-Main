About Me

About

I am a young creative who has found his love for coding after many years in varying fields. I have a love for the creative arts and culture, and I have found this to be a big influence on my work as a full-stack developer. My broad skillset and background in other fields has given me a deeper understanding of how I can create functional, accessible and compliant software, programs and web content for my clients. On top of this I find these skills to be of great value when consulting on projects within other fields such as Construction and Health & Fitness. When I am not working doing development work, I enjoy spending my time outdoors climbing, playing guitar, photography and editing, painting, playing board games/video games or strength training.
